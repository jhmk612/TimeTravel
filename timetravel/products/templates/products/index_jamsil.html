Your Journey is our priority

Located in Songpa-gu, Seoul, Jamsil earned nationwide fame during the 1988 Olympics. Afterwards, the sports complex has been hosting numerous sports and entertainment events. If you wish to experience some of the world class concerts and historical sports events, Jamsil offers you the best moments you can imagine. Also, the two Lotteworlds, with their theme parks and shopping centers, can provide a pleasant and exciting experience. Take a walk around Seokchon Lake and relax!

Exciting Course

Be part of the historical 1988 Seoul Olympics!
WHERE :

Jamsil Sports Complex

TIME PERIOD :

September 17th ~ October 2nd, 1988

A haze of excitement surrounded Jamsil during 1988 due to the first Olympics that was held in Republic of Korea. Choose any sport events that interests you. Or you can just feel the atmosphere of the event. Watch the stars of the entire Oylmpics with your own eyes. If you are tired from the heat of the events, you can take a walk down the Olympic Park to relax.

Feel the people's excitement as world's best atheltes competed each other!

Relaxing Course

Missed Rubber Duck?
WHERE :

Seokchon Lake

TIME PERIOD :

October 14th ~ November 14th, 2014

The Rubber Duck project, a 1 ton rubber duck art display by Florentijn Hofman, has attracted more than 4.8 million people during the display period. If you hadn't been able to take a picture with the cute duck before, here is your chance! Relax your mind by looking at the peaceful scenery of a cute duck floating about the lake.

Take your time around the lovely lake and relax your mind by looking at the cute scenery!

Price : $250
