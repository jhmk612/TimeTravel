Gwanghwamun is the main and largest gate of Gyeongbokgung Palace, in Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea. It is located at a three-way intersection at the northern end of Sejongno. As a landmark and symbol of Seoul's long history as the capital city during the Joseon Dynasty, the gate has gone through multiple periods of destruction and disrepair. Restoration work on the gate was finished and it was opened to the public on August 15, 2010.

Exciting Course

Be part of the history of 3.1 ceremony of Korean Independence!
WHERE :

INSADONG -> TABGOL PARK -> BUKCHON

TIME PERIOD :

MARCH 1st, 1919

Duration :

5~6 hr

March 1st, 1919, Hanyang(Seoul) was full of shoutings "Mansei" for the independence of Korea. Through this course, you can join and fully experience the meaningful historical event.

First, being in Tae-Hwa-Gwan, Insadong, you can watch 33 ethnic representatives having the ceremony for the Independence of Korea.

Second, visit Tabgol Park, where the suffered and the colonized by the forced invasion of Japan held their declaration of independence. Prepare a Korean National Flag. Holding the same flag all together and shouting the independence of Korea, you are being a part of the heart of the history of Korea.

Last, visiting Bukchon at this moment is truly meaningful. Since the protesters marched out through the whole city, especially in Bukchon and Jongro.

Join the History of Korea's Independence!

Price : $500

Amusing Course

A trip to experience contemporary protests of Korea!
WHERE :

Gwanghwamun Plaza

TIME PERIOD :

Dec 17, 2016 -> Mar 10, 2017-> Mar 11, 2017

Duration :

4~5 hr

After discovering the corruption of the government and the fact of 'ChoiSunSil Gate', citizens congregated in the Gwanghwamun Plaza, and shouted for the impeachment of the president Park Geun Hye.

First, Dec 17, 2016, the Constitutional Court received the case of judging the President wheter to impeach her or not. On that day, approximately 1 million people assembled. Through the trip, you can feel the bond and be part of them again.

Second, Mar 10, 2017, 11 a.m., the judgment of impeaching the President was declared. "We will dismiss Park Geun-hye, the accused." Do you want to hear it once again with the people? Let's get on the trip!

Last, Mar 11, 2017, Impeachment Celebration Fireworks Festival was held. In front of Gwanghwamun, people congregated once again, celebrating themselves for the impeachment. You can feel the excitement once again.

Join the History of Korea's Contemporary History!

Price : $300

Relaxing Course

Join our Trip to the accession of Korean King, Gojong!
WHERE :

Deoksoogung Palace

TIME PERIOD :

1863

Duration :

2~3 hr

Gojong, the king who went through the Japanese Colonial Era ascended the throne in 1863, Deoksoogung Palace.

First, watch and follow the parade of Korean King toward the Deoksoogung Palace.

Second, walk around and experience the palace in the 19C. You can compare and contrast what it looks like as you walk by.

Last, watch all the people around Jongro celebrating and blessing the King Gojong.

Never met the Korean King before? Perhaps this is your last chance! Meet the King Gojong, while you take a walk for Deoksoogung Palace.
