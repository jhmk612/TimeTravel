Ioannes Paulus PP. II

Finding Dispersed Families

Yeouido Plaza

Your Journey is our priority

Yeouido, located in Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul, is the central place of Korean politics and finance. Until 1999, Yeouido Plaza, now Yeouido Park, with its capacity to host millions of people, had been the place for holding national events. Also riding a bike or meeting friends or lovers on the plaza had been one of the major activities for people of 1980s and 90s.

Religous Course

Experience what it had been like when the pope was there.
WHERE :

Yeouido Plaza

TIME PERIOD :

October 5th, 1989

Duration :

5~6 hr

On october 5th, 1989, Pope Ioannes Paulus PP. II visited Korea and addressed 650,000 people in Yeouido with a message of peace and hope. Be a part of this extraordinary event and reflect upon how we can live.

Join one of the most meaningful events that have passed our lives.

Price : $500

Dramatic Course

When dispersed families reunite again.
WHERE :

Yeouido Plaza

TIME PERIOD :

June 30th, 1983 ~ Novemeber 14th, 1983

Duration :

4~5 hr

KBS Special Live Broadcasting 'Finding Dispersed Families' was the largest dispersed families reunification program in the world with 100,952 submitted cases, 53,536 broadcasted and 10,189 families reunited during 138 days of broadcasting.

Many families and viewers alike rejoiced and mourned for each reunification and loss during the days.

Be a part of this dramatic event and lend your emotional support.

Price : $300

Relaxing Course

Take a walk along the Yeouido Plaza, where people met and played.
WHERE :

Yeouido Plaza

TIME PERIOD :

1980s ~ 1995

Duration :

2~3 hr

When Yeouido Plaza wasn't being used as the holding place of national events, it was one of the best places where families and friends could spend their time together during the weekends. People used to ride bicycles and rollerskates around the plaza. Experience what peaceuful times during the 1980s had been like in the plaza.

You might even see your parents meeting each other here. Search for familiar faces and the retro fashion for real!

Price : $250
